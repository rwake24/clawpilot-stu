Capabilities

What it does

📝

Meeting notes → Obsidian

Pulls Teams transcripts, drafts the note, tags it, and links the account page. No copy/paste.

📇

Account prep on demand

One ask → state of play, opportunity snapshot, push points, and watch-outs in your preferred format.

📧

Email triage & action tracking

Sweeps Outlook, Teams, and meeting notes for open AIs. One running list, write-back when done.

📊

MSX hygiene & scorecards

Risk-bucketed sweeps, pipeline GPA, and milestone updates without opening Dynamics.

🤖

Custom skills & automations

Heartbeats, scheduled briefs, and your own slash-commands. Teach it once, reuse forever.

🔗

Connects to your stack

M365, MSX, Obsidian, Power BI, the browser, your filesystem. If it has an API, it's fair game.
